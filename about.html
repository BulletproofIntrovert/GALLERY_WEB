About Me

Hi, I'm a self-taught photographer and video editor. My biggest passion is a creative documentary, street photography and photojournalism. I used to work as a correspondent in the Lithuanian Newspapers but have noticed that I more enjoy telling stories through images or video. I like to caught natural moments, details or emotions which have a message and makes people think. If you like a documentary style of photography you can hire me for public, professional or personal events.
I'm working with Canon D500 camera (which I absolutely love) and few lenses: Cannon 40mm and 85mm.
Images editing with Adobe Photoshop.
Video editing with Adobe Premiere or Final Cut.
